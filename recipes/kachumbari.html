Kachumbari, Kenyan salad

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes and sliced onion.
  2. Add the chopped green chili if using.
  3. Squeeze the lemon juice over the mixture and season with salt.
  4. Toss everything together gently.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  6. Serve immediately as a side dish or salad.
